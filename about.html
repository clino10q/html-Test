About RYA

Welcome to a remarkable petroleum company from the heart of Africa to the World!

RYA... powering every mile, fueling every future.

At RYA, we are driven by passion to fuel progress and empower communities. Since 1980, we have delivered reliable and affordable energy solutions, exceeding expectations with exceptional services and products.

RYA has been a cornerstone of Nigeria’s energy landscape. We are more than just an energy company; we are a dedicated partner invested in the well-being of the communities we serve.

Our mission is to provide sustainable energy solutions that improve lives and impact our world by driving progress. This drives us towards building formidable teams who exemplify professionalism, innovation, excellence, integrity and empathy, so as to always be your most trusted energy service provider.

We are constantly improving our services and products by ensuring:
Unwavering Commitment to Quality: Delivering exceptional petroleum products that meet the highest international standards.
Investing in People: Building strong teams and fostering a rewarding culture of excellence.
Exceptional Service: Being the best by going above and beyond to ensure customer satisfaction.


We believe in responsible energy production that benefits not only our community but also our country, continent and the planet. That is why we are always evolving to be your trusted one-stop energy solutions company.

Discover our story and explore the exciting possibilities we are creating.
Join us on our journey towards building a future of endless possibilities where we empower lives through sustainable energy solutions.

Our History

Rya was founded in 1976 with a clear vision to deliver reliable and efficient energy solutions. Starting with a focus on fuel distribution, the company quickly grew by building strong partnerships and maintaining high standards of quality and service.

The journey began with Dr. Ilelaboye Ayomide, Chief Operations Officer of RYA Group. As a dealer under Systems Technologies Ltd., Dr. Ayomide’s exceptional sales performance and innovative marketing strategies earned her the Uniform PLC. Best Dealer Award for the Latin American/West African Region. This groundbreaking achievement marked him as the first Nigerian to attain such recognition, setting the stage for RYA’s future success.

Over time, Rya expanded into lubricants, bulk storage, and marine logistics, strengthening its presence across the energy value chain. Today, we continue to grow by embracing innovation, investing in infrastructure, and staying committed to powering progress in every community we serve.
